Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

We work on a wide range of projects including corporate events, weddings, brand photography, promotional videos, social media content, and post-production editing for clients of all sizes.

How do I request a quote?

You can request a quote through our online form or contact us directly. We'll discuss your project needs and provide a custom quote within 1-2 business days.

What's your turnaround time?

Turnaround time is project dependent. Each project has unique requirements and complexity, so we'll provide a specific timeline when discussing your project details.

Do you travel for projects?

Travel is possible but dependent on the project. We're based in Hampton Roads, Virginia, and travel fees would apply for projects outside our local service area.

What equipment do you use?

We use professional-grade equipment including multiple camera bodies, lenses, lighting setups, audio equipment, and industry-standard editing software. Equipment selection is tailored to each project to ensure high-quality deliverables.
