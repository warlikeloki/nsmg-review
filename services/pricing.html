Pricing FAQs

Do you offer package discounts?

Yes! Our packages bundle multiple services at discounted rates compared to à la carte pricing. Packages are ideal for events requiring photography, videography, and editing services.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely. We can customize any package to fit your specific needs. Contact us to discuss your project requirements and we'll create a tailored quote.

What's included in the hourly rate?

Hourly rates include the service time, professional equipment, and standard post-production. Additional editing, rush delivery, or special requests may incur extra charges.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, we typically require a 50% deposit to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due upon project completion or delivery of final files.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, checks, and most major credit cards. Payment details will be provided in your service agreement.
